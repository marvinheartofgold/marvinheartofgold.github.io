Our Services

Marymount Centre provides the following services. We support our residents through a comprehensive range of programmes to help them work through their problems and trauma and to rebuild their lives.

Ahuva Good Shepherd (AGS) runs three residential homes for girls.

Read more

Good Shepherd Centre (GSC) offers a safe place for women and their children experiencing family violence, and other women facing seemingly insurmountable difficulties in their lives. Through a wide range of programmes to support them in their healing, we empower them to tap on their inner strength and resources to rebuild their lives and create a future for themselves and their families. GSC is appointed by the Singapore Government to provide victim care for women who have been trafficked into Singapore.

Read more

Good Shepherd Student Care (GSSC) provides after-school care for students at Marymount Convent School while their parents are at work. Some students are from vulnerable families, such as single-parent and low-income households.
