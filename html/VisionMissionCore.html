Our Vision, Mission and Core Values


Our Vision

Touching Lives, Building Hope

Our Mission

Empowering Lives, Restoring Rights, Upholding Dignity

We believe in the inherent dignity and value of each person, and we promote a culture of safety, respect and responsibility. We are committed to reaching out with compassion and respect to women and children who are experiencing injustices or problems that are too heavy to face alone. We journey with them in the healing of their personhood and empower them to rebuild their lives so that they can restore their sense of dignity and worth.

Our Core Values

  • Compassion
  • Integrity
  • Empowerment
  • Professionalism
  • Teamwork
  • Respect
  • Zeal
