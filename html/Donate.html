To request for tax deduction for contributions via PayNow, please indicate your full name (as in your NRIC or UEN registration) under Bill Reference. Please also email us at admin@marymountctr.org.sg with your full name, NRIC or UEN number, the date of donation and donation amount.

Cash or Cheque Donations

For cash donations, please email us at admin@marymountctr.org.sg or call us at 6256 4440 on Mondays to Fridays, 10 am to 5 pm to arrange for us to receive your cash donation.

For cheque donations, please make your cheque payable to Marymount Centre and mail to:

Marymount Centre
9 Lorong 8 Toa Payoh
Good Shepherd Place
Singapore 319253

If you would like to request for tax deduction, please indicate your full name (as in your NRIC or UEN registration) and NRIC or UEN number on the back of the cheque.

In‐Kind Donations

We appreciate in‐kind donation items. Please email us at admin@marymountctr.org.sg or call us at 6256 4440 on Mondays to Fridays, 10 am to 5 pm to arrange for us to receive your in-kind donation items.

Legacy Donations

Legacy donations can be made through your will by leaving a bequest to Marymount Centre, or through your CPF by nominating Marymount Centre as a beneficiary of your CPF savings, or you can designate Marymount Centre as a beneficiary of your insurance policy. For more information, please email us at admin@marymountctr.org.sg or call us at 6256 4440 on Mondays to Fridays, 10 am to 5 pm.
