Volunteer With Us

Over the years, many volunteers have extended their willing hands and given us their loving support. They have touched the lives of the women and children under our care. We are indeed very grateful for their help.

We look forward to welcoming you as a volunteer. Please contact ahuva@marymountctr.org.sg.

Service Based

We conduct various activities to support and empower our beneficiaries. Join our staff in carrying out these activities or initiate a new activity to bless our beneficiaries. Examples include:

  • Tuition and Teaching
    • Supervise and guide our beneficiaries in their studies and help them prepare for their exams
    • Conduct English language and other classes for our beneficiaries
  • Arts and Craft
    • Spark the imagination of our beneficiaries through arts and craft activities
  • Music and Dance
    • Unleash the creative energy of our beneficiaries through music and dance
  • Speech and Drama
    • Develop the confidence of our beneficiaries through speech and drama
  • Life Skills
    • Teach our beneficiaries cooking, baking, sewing, time and money management skills
  • Grooming and Wellness
    • Pass on to our beneficiaries grooming and make-up tips on how to look presentable for school or job interviews
    • Help our beneficiaries to self-regulate through counselling and well-being programmes such as meditation and yoga
  • Sports and Fitness
    • Help our beneficiaries to stay fit and healthy
    • Help our beneficiaries develop character and leadership skills and teach them teamwork
Events Based

We require additional manpower support when we organise outings and events (such as festive celebrations) for our beneficiaries.

We also need help to source for sponsorships for such activities or to defray the fees payable to third party programme and other providers.

Skills Based

Help us strengthen our capabilities by offering your talent and experience in areas such as photography, videography, graphic design, web development and emceeing.
